What is flopple ?

flopple is a parody of Wordle based on the meme "Big Floppa".
New on flopple ? Create an account !

How to play ?

Create an account and then choose the number of letters and the number of tries you want
You will earn more if you take some risks !
Try to guess the word based on the letter colors (I know you already know how Wordle works)
Win and you will earn some Floppa Coins
Lose, and you will get a Bingus fragment
We WILL delete your account if you get 3 fragments
You can spend your coins in the vending machine to get some rare caracal images
The more you spend, the more it is likely that you will get something rare
You can then go to the gallery to see your collection
